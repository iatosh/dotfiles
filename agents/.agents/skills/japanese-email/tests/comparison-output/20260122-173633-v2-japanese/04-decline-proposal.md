### Structure Explanation:

| Section | Japanese | Purpose |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Subject | ご提案の件につきまして | "Regarding your proposal" |
| Opening | 拝啓・平素は格別の... | Formal greeting and gratitude for ongoing relationship |
| Thanks | ご提案をいただき... | Thank them for the proposal |
| Decision | 誠に恐縮ではございますが... | Politely decline using humble language (謙譲語) |
| Apology | せっかくのお申し出に... | Express regret for not accepting |
| Future | 今後とも...また別の機会... | Maintain relationship for future opportunities |
| Closing | 敬具 | Formal closing |
| Signature | 会社名・部署・名前・連絡先 | Company and contact information |
