## Structure Explanation:

1. **件名 (Subject):** Simple and clear
2. **宛名 (Addressee):** Company, department, and name with 様
3. **挨拶 (Greeting):** Standard business opening expressing gratitude for the ongoing relationship
4. **感謝 (Thanks):** Thanking them for the project request and expressing honor
5. **断り (Decline):** Politely explaining capacity constraints with humble language (恐縮ながら, 叶わない状況)
6. **謝罪 (Apology):** Sincere apology for not being able to help
7. **代替案 (Alternative):** Introducing ABC株式会社 as a trusted partner with a recommendation
8. **提案 (Offer):** Offering to make an introduction if desired
9. **結び (Closing):** Final apology and request for continued relationship
10. **署名 (Signature):** Standard business signature block

The email uses appropriate keigo (敬語) and follows Japanese business email conventions for declining while preserving the relationship.
